Content Strategy: My PWP will be a personal portfolio that will consist of one page, and split into a few different sections. I'd also like to provide a good color palette for the site, to make sure everything looks synchronized, including any images.

The first section will be the Welcome area. This will include my name, and my job description of "Full Stack Web Developer" overlaid across a piece of my photography.

The secondary section will be the About Me section. Here, I want to include some of my accomplishments, some of my skills, and past job history. Within this, I'd like to show off some of my talents and experiences that I can bring to a workplace environment and make sure employers are aware of my strengths. I'd also like to include a portrait of myself here.

The third section will consist of the Portfolio of my work. In this area, I want to provide a carousel of the projects I've worked on, and what I did within this projects. I'd probably like to include 3-4 examples of things that I consider to be my most exemplary work.

The fourth section will include the Contact Me form. Here, I'll allow employers to contact me via email, and include a message. I'll also include some brief info on a response time to the right as well, and some links to some other places you can see my projects and social media.
